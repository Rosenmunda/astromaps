Celestial Atlas

Modern Astronomy · Vedic Wisdom

The Idea: To merge the geometric precision of modern astronomical mapping with the profound spiritual depth of ancient Vedic astrology (Jyotisha).

The Purpose: To provide a culturally immersive and scientifically accurate tool for stargazers and seekers alike, allowing for a dual exploration of the heavens through both astrophysical data and traditional Sanskrit scriptures.

The Aim: To preserve and present the 88 IAU constellations alongside their Nakshatra mappings, Devanagari mantras, and auspicious attributes—creating a living bridge between the physical sky and the metaphysical soul.
